🔄 Refund & Return Policy

Effective from {{ now().strftime('%B %d, %Y') }}

✅ Eligibility for Refunds & Returns

  • Items must be unused, unopened, and in original packaging.
  • Returns accepted within 7 days of delivery.
  • Perfumes must remain sealed unless covered by our fragrance guarantee (see below).
  • Watches must be unworn with box, warranty card, and all accessories.

🌟 Our Fragrance Promise

At Bin Ahmed, we want you to love your fragrance.
If you don’t like the fragrance after purchase, you may return it within 7 days for a replacement or refund — no questions asked.

🚫 Non-Returnable Items

  • Perfumes that are half-used or bottles intentionally damaged.
  • Products damaged by mishandling, water, or normal wear & tear.
  • Customized or limited-edition products.

💰 Refund Process

Once we receive and inspect your return, you’ll be notified of the approval or rejection. Approved refunds are processed within 7–10 business days via your original payment method.
Note: Shipping charges are non-refundable unless the issue was caused by us.

🔄 Exchanges

Exchanges are only available for items that are defective or damaged upon arrival. Contact us immediately with your order details if you need a replacement.

📩 How to Initiate a Return

Simply email us at support@binahmed.com with your order number, product details, and reason for return. Our support team will respond quickly and guide you through the process.
