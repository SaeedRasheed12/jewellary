Privacy & Data Protection Policy

Last updated {{ now().strftime('%B %d, %Y') }}

At Aurielle, your privacy is as valuable as the jewels we craft. This policy explains how we collect, protect, and use your information to ensure a safe and transparent experience while shopping with us.

📦 What Information We Collect

  • Personal details: Name, email, phone number, and shipping address.
  • Payment data: Processed securely through trusted gateways — we never store card details.
  • Browsing info: IP address, device type, and cookies for site performance and personalization.
  • Communication history: Messages, reviews, or customer support inquiries.

🔒 How We Protect Your Data

  • All transactions are encrypted using SSL (Secure Socket Layer) technology.
  • We use advanced firewalls and regular security audits to safeguard your information.
  • Access to customer data is strictly limited to authorized Aurielle staff only.
  • Your personal data is never sold or shared with third parties without your consent.

🎯 Why We Use Your Information

  • To process and deliver your orders accurately and efficiently.
  • To send important updates, offers, and newsletters (only with your permission).
  • To enhance our website experience using anonymous analytics and preferences.
  • To provide faster support and personalized jewelry recommendations.

🍪 Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses cookies to improve your browsing experience. These small data files help us remember your preferences and shopping cart contents. You can disable cookies anytime in your browser settings — though some features may not function properly without them.

🤝 Sharing with Trusted Partners

We may share limited data with verified third parties (e.g., courier or payment providers) only for order fulfillment and customer service — never for advertising or resale purposes.

🧾 Your Rights & Choices

  • Request access to your personal data or corrections at any time.
  • Opt-out of promotional emails through the unsubscribe link.
  • Request complete deletion (“right to be forgotten”) by contacting us directly.

📧 Contact Us

If you have any questions about this policy or your data, please contact us at privacy@aurielle.com . Our team is always happy to assist and protect your trust.

Your trust is our most precious gem. Thank you for letting Aurielle be a part of your story. 💎
