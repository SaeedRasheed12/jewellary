Delivery & Exchange Policy

At Aurielle, we ensure your precious order arrives safely and on time. Standard delivery takes 2 – 7 business days across Pakistan.

  • If your parcel doesn’t arrive within 7 days, please contact us via WhatsApp (link on homepage) for instant assistance.
  • Delivery timelines may vary slightly for custom or pre-order pieces.

Exchange Policy

  • Exchange available within 24 hours of delivery for unused, unworn jewelry.
  • Items must be returned in their original packaging with all tags, boxes, and pouches intact.
  • Customized, engraved, or sale items are not eligible for exchange.
  • Please inform us via WhatsApp before initiating an exchange.
  • Exchange shipping charges are covered by the customer unless the item is defective or incorrect.

For any queries, contact our official WhatsApp (shown on homepage). We’re always here to help — your satisfaction is our sparkle.
